Follow along with this recipe

  • 4 pita bread
  • 6 oz . tomato sauce
  • 2 small tomatoes
  • 1 cup lettuce
  • 3/4 cup fresh basil
  • 6 oz . fresh mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Top each pita bread with tomato sauce, lettuce, tomato slices, basil, and fresh mozzarella
  3. Bake on a baking sheet covered in foil paper for 5-7 minutes
  4. Remove from oven and enjoy
